Learn Sign Language

Our Learn Sign Language class helps church members communicate with the deaf community through practical lessons and Bible-centered fellowship.

Sessions include basic vocabulary, common worship terms, and real conversation practice so you can confidently welcome and serve others.

Who can join: Teens and adults of all skill levels.

What to bring: A notebook and a willing heart to learn.
