How to Use This Website

Find the right page quickly and know what to click next.

Start Here

This website is designed to help new visitors, church members, and families find important information fast. Use the menu at the top of the page on desktop, or tap the Menu button on your phone to open the site navigation.

1. Open the Menu

Use the top navigation to explore the main sections. On mobile, tap the Menu button to see all the links.

2. Choose Your Goal

Each page is built around a specific task like planning a visit, asking for prayer, learning about ministries, or contacting the church.

3. Use the Buttons

Look for buttons such as Plan Your Visit, Contact Us, or Give to move directly to the next step.

Use these quick paths if you already know what you need.

See service times, what to expect, and where the church is located before you come.

Go to Plan Your Visit

Get Directions

Share a Prayer Request

Submit a Prayer Request

Fill Out a Visitor Card

Give Online

Helpful Tips

Using a Phone

Tap the Menu button at the top of the page to open the full navigation. Tap any link inside the menu to go directly to that page.

Opening Outside Links

Some links, such as Facebook, may open in a new tab so you can keep your place on the church website.

Using Forms

On contact and prayer pages, fill in the required fields and press the submit button. Keep your email or phone number accurate if you want a reply.
